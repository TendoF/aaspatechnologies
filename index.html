FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer a warranty on all products?
+
Yes. Every laptop, desktop, and tablet we sell comes with a full 1-year warranty. If anything goes wrong within that period, we handle repairs or replacement at no extra cost.
Do you deliver outside Harare?
+
Absolutely. We deliver nationwide across Zimbabwe including Bulawayo, Gweru, Mutare, Masvingo, and smaller towns. Contact us via WhatsApp to confirm delivery timelines and costs for your area.
What payment methods do you accept?
+
We accept EcoCash, ZiG, USD cash, bank transfer, and Innbucks. Speak to us on WhatsApp to confirm which method works best for your order.
Can I get a quote for a business order?
+
Yes, and we actively encourage it. We offer discounted pricing for bulk orders and full business ICT packages. WhatsApp us or fill in the contact form and we'll send you a detailed quote within 24 hours.
Are the products new or refurbished?
+
We carry a mix of brand-new and professionally refurbished (grade A) products. All refurbished units are thoroughly tested, cleaned, and come with the same 1-year warranty. Each product listing clearly states its condition.
How long does data recovery take?
+
Data recovery typically takes 24–72 hours depending on the severity of the issue. For logical failures (deleted files, formatting) it's often same-day. Physical drive failures may take longer. We'll give you an honest assessment when you bring the drive in.
